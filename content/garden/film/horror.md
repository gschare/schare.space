# Horror Movie Tuesdays, Fall 2025
See [List on Letterboxd](https://boxd.it/PWMXG).

We adopt a strongly pluralist attitude toward what qualifies as horror.

<details>
<summary>
Potential Watchlist. [Suggest a movie!](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1K8z1cD-bz_aaM8xCVaPoRVE4rLUGe2HbpY0bnoKQvec/edit?usp=sharing)
</summary>

**bold** = particularly exciting to me

- Psychological horror
  - **Rosemary's Baby**
  - Black Swan
  - anything by David Lynch
  - Killing of A Sacred Deer
- Classic B-movie horror
  - The Blob
  - Invasion of the Body Snatchers
  - They Live - this is more of an action movie
  - Plan 9 From Outer Space - quite possibly the worst movie I've ever seen
  - Phase IV
- Body horror
  - **Possession**
  - anything by Cronenberg (e.g. **Videodrome**)
  - [x] The Substance
- Monster
  - Jaws
  - It Follows
  - Vampire
    - Nosferatu
    - Bram Stoker's Dracula
    - Twilight (save it for Robby Patty series)
    - Interview with the Vampire
  - Zombie movies
    - **Night of the Living Dead**
    - Dawn of the Dead
    - Shaun of the Dead
    - **28 Days Later** (my favorite movie)
    - Train to Busan
    - World War Z
    - I Am Legend
    - **Warm Bodies**
    - Cargo
    - Poison
    - lots of TV shows in this category: The Walking Dead, The Last of Us
- Slasher
  - The Shining, for an elevated instance
  - **Halloween**
  - **Scream**
  - **Nightmare on Elm Street**
  - Psycho
- A24
  - Hereditary
  - Lamb
  - [x] The Witch
  - [x] Saint Maud
- Sci-fi horror
  - **Alien**
  - Cube
  - [x] **The Thing**
  - Annihilation
- Cozy or funny horror
  - **Corpse Bride**
  - The Nightmare Before Christmas
  - Halloweentown
  - Beetlejuice
  - House by Obayashi
  - Big Bad Wolves
- Contemporary
  - **Barbarian**
  - I Saw The TV Glow
  - **Weapons**
  - Us
- **Silence of the Lambs**
- **The Exorcist**
- Pan's Labyrinth
- Cape Fear
- Sijjin
- Cuckoo
- A Girl Walks Home Alone At Night
- Under the Shadow

</details>
