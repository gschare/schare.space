Intentional Communities / Housing Cooperatives

Terminology

  • co-op/cooperative: autonomous or semi-autonomous community of people living together in a communal space with shared responsibilities
  • coliving: corporate-owned for-profit space with cleaning staff and communal amenities
  • commune: autonomous community that is mostly self-sufficient with high degree of communal responsibility
  • cohousing: sort of a catch-all that includes more casual attempts to form community in regular apartments
