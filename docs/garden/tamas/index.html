reality is a rocky shore for a driftwood dreamer

It was crazy before.
Here it stands, a vast
and lonely inter-net.
No strength of will to perform
in guise an uncompelling form;
indifference is not the word---
too far to fathom, such are
the insufficiencies revealed.
Not prohibition nor passion,
neither missive nor permission.
It's entirely up to you.




    Only off to wander /
    Look at that flower!
