That I am here now is a tautology does not detract from its truth or its wisdom.

Focus is a muscle. The body obeys.

At the time of solstice, do not travel through the provinces.

Real-time collaboration hits like crack.

Communion and daily bread are not Christian rituals.

If it hurts... don't do it!

Nostalgia is like envy: comparison is an invitation to tend your own garden.

It helps to have a direction.

A thousand miles an hour in a thousand directions.

It seems as if chance were at work.

Ged said: The one power that is real and worth the having is the power, not to take, but to accept.

Ogion the Silent said: To hear, one must be silent.

Pain is inevitable. Suffering is optional.

History happens behind the backs of its participants.

Success is doing what you must.

Take the lively air, and, lovely, learn by going where I have to go.

The mystery of life is not a problem to solve but a reality to experience.

The wind rises! We must try to live!
